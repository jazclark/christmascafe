About the Christmas Cafe

Who and What

Mr and Mrs Clause are delighted to announce their (semi) retirement from the Toy Delivery service as Santa Clause Jr takes over the family business! Though with years and years of hard work, the Clause's aren't ready to give up the working life just yet.

The Christmas Cafe is the perfect stop for your morning cup of Jo on your way into The Workshop, or a great local hang out to keep up with the local happenings.
